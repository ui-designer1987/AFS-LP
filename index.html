What is Amity
Finishing School?

Amity Finishing School is India’s No.1 residential school specializing in International Business Etiquette and Protocol Training. AFS offers a unique blend of theoretical and practical learning designed to enhance confidence, social finesse, and professional prowess. Students graduate as poised individuals equipped to excel in both social and business environments.

Who Can Join the Course?

The Amity Finishing School is ideal for:

  • Men and women who want to enhance their confidence in Personal & Professional scenario.
  • Students (16+ years), eager to hone their social and professional skills.
  • Professionals seeking to polish their personal brand and gain an edge in the workplace.
  • Entrepreneurs who desire to elevate their business etiquette and make impactful first impressions.
  • Anyone looking to refine their personality with charm, finesse, and sophistication.
